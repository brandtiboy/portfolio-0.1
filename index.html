About me

I solve users problems through user-centric design.

I just graduated University with a MSc in Webcommunication and Design from Syddansk Universitet in Kolding, Denmark. My most recent work was my Master thesis, where we wanted to investigate generation z and their use of the internet and how the gaming- and streaming industries have adopted to that behaviour and used it to their advantage.

After the research of disruption and an analysis of generation z and the gaming- and streaming industries, we established five tendencies. They were tested on two business cases using a Business Model Canvas. Some of the tendencies actually predicted the near future and therefore potentially could secure businesses from suffering from a potential disruption in their industry.

Througout the University i have worked on several human-centred projects with both theory, deliverables and design methods. I love working with and for the users. My master thesis also showcased how much power the users have today and that user-generated platforms have a huge success.
